The Location

East Boston,
at its best.

Havre Street sits in the core of East Boston — walkable, connected, and genuinely community-driven. One of the best value plays left in the city.

  • Blue Line · 5 min walk

    Direct to downtown, Back Bay, and Logan Airport. No car required.

  • Harborwalk & Piers Park

    East Boston's waterfront is a few blocks away — waterfront runs, morning walks, summer events.

  • Dining & Community

    Some of Boston's best Latin food, neighborhood coffee shops, and local restaurants line Meridian and Maverick Square.

  • Appreciation Story

    East Boston has seen consistent appreciation driven by transit access, waterfront investment, and proximity to downtown Boston.

Questions

Things buyers
want to know.

We'd rather you have the answers upfront than wonder. Reach out anytime for anything not covered here.

Your purchase and sale agreement includes provisions for reasonable delays. We will communicate any material changes to the timeline directly to all buyers as soon as we know. Our current Q4 2025 estimate is our best projection based on current progress and conditions.
Walkthroughs are typically scheduled 2–4 weeks prior to closing. You'll walk your unit with our project manager to identify any punch list items before your move-in date. A second walkthrough is done after punch list items are addressed to confirm completion.
Depending on where we are in construction, there may be opportunities to select from a curated upgrade package. Buyers will be contacted individually about selection deadlines. Changes made after certain construction milestones may not be possible.
Your primary point of contact is the sales team. For construction-specific questions, we hold periodic buyer update calls and send bi-weekly field journal emails. Use the form on this site to register and ensure you're on our list.
All units include a standard 1-year builder's warranty covering workmanship and materials, plus manufacturer warranties on all appliances and mechanical systems. Massachusetts law also provides additional implied warranties for new residential construction.
Registered buyers receive a bi-weekly field journal email with site photos and a brief construction note. This site is also updated at each major milestone. We believe in over-communicating rather than going quiet.
